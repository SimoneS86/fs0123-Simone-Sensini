Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new fsvorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the Focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 talespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brudhing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desidered, tought it's notnecessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a padlle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, tought i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
